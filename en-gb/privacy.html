1) Data controller & contact

The controller of the Site is VPN World (site owner). For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact us:

We do not appoint a DPO as this is not required for our processing scale.

2) What we collect (and what we don’t)

  • No invasive analytics (we don’t use Google Analytics or profiling cookies).
  • GitHub Pages may keep standard server logs (IP, user-agent, timestamp, URL) for security and troubleshooting. We don’t merge logs to identify you.
  • If you email us, we process your message and address to reply.
  • We do not ask you to register or create accounts on the Site.

3) Legal bases

  • Legitimate interests — site security, prevention of abuse, basic aggregate metrics.

  • Contract/Pre-contract — to respond to your enquiry.

6) Retention

  • Consent choices: up to 12 months unless you clear them earlier.
