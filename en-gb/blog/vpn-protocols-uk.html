TL;DR — which protocol to use

  • Daily UK use: WireGuard/NordLynx — best speed & responsiveness on broadband/5G.
  • Restrictive networks: OpenVPN TCP/443 — “looks like HTTPS”, works in many locked-down environments.
  • Roaming on iPhone/iPad: IKEv2 — very stable handoffs; fine for mobile privacy and commuting.

For full app presets see Best VPN Settings (UK) and leak checks in IP/DNS Leaks — UK.

WireGuard / NordLynx

Why UK users love it: exceptional speeds, low CPU overhead, quick reconnects on flaky Wi-Fi/5G. NordLynx is NordVPN’s WireGuard adaptation with an extra NAT layer for account privacy.

ProsCons
  • Fastest protocol in most UK cases
  • Great for 4K streaming and cloud gaming
  • Quick to reconnect after sleep/roaming
  • Some office/campus networks may throttle/flag UDP
  • Manual router configs require care (keys, MTU)

Tip: if you see packet loss or captive portals, temporarily switch to OpenVPN TCP/443, then return to WireGuard for speed.

OpenVPN (UDP vs TCP/443)

OpenVPN UDP is the classic default — efficient and widely supported. OpenVPN TCP/443 runs over the same port as HTTPS and often succeeds on restrictive UK networks that block/shape UDP.

ModeBest useNotes
UDPGeneral use when the network is not restrictiveLower overhead than TCP; good performance
TCP/443Public Wi-Fi, campuses, offices with strict firewallsResilient, but a bit slower; great fallback for streaming access

For Netflix/iPlayer errors, TCP/443 + UK server rotation helps. See the playbooks: Netflix UK, BBC iPlayer.

IKEv2/IPsec

Beloved on iOS for stable roaming between Wi-Fi and mobile data. It’s simple and battery-friendly, but not ideal against aggressive enterprise firewalls. Use it when you prioritise stable, quick reconnects on the move.

  • Pros: fast handoffs, low overhead on mobiles
  • Cons: can be blocked or inspected; fewer tweak options

Streaming in the UK (Netflix, BBC iPlayer)

  • Start with WireGuard for speed; if you see proxy errors, switch to OpenVPN TCP/443 and rotate UK endpoints.
  • Relaunch the app after each change; clear Smart TV app data if it clings to an old region.
  • Router users: follow VPN Router Setup (UK) and check Best VPN Settings for split tunneling.

FAQ

Which protocol is fastest in the UK?

WireGuard/NordLynx in most cases on broadband/5G.

What if my office Wi-Fi blocks VPNs?

Try OpenVPN TCP/443 — it usually slips through strict firewalls.

Is IKEv2 still worth using?

Yes for iOS roaming and stability; not ideal against aggressive filters.

Will switching protocol fix streaming?

Often. Combine protocol switch with UK server rotation and app relaunch. See our Netflix and iPlayer guides.
