FAQ (UK)

Is using a VPN on iPhone legal in the UK?

Yes. Using a VPN is legal in the UK. A VPN is a privacy and security tool; what matters is how you use it (e.g., don’t break platform terms or laws).

Will a VPN drain my iPhone battery?

Modern protocols like WireGuard typically add a small overhead. In practice, many users see roughly a few percent extra drain per hour during active use; OpenVPN is usually heavier.

Can I watch BBC iPlayer abroad on iOS with a VPN?

Often yes — but you need a provider with reliable UK streaming servers. Use WireGuard, choose a UK server (London/Manchester), disable Location Services for iPlayer, and restart the app after connecting.

Why do some apps still show my real location with a VPN on?

VPNs change your IP routing, but apps can still use GPS, Wi‑Fi positioning, and device signals. If an app matters, limit its Location Services permissions and retest.

Do I need a router VPN if I already use iOS VPN?

Not required, but helpful for consoles/Smart TVs and whole‑home consistency. See our router setup guide if you want one VPN for every device.
