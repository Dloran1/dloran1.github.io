Quick setup (3 minutes)

  1. Install your VPN app (NordVPN, Surfshark or ExpressVPN) from Google Play.
  2. Log in → set Protocol: WireGuard / NordLynx.
  3. Enable Kill switch and Auto-connect on Wi-Fi & mobile data.
  4. Connect to a nearby UK server (London, Manchester, Birmingham).

Need desktop too? See VPN for Windows and VPN for iOS.

Best Android VPN settings for the UK

  • Protocol: WireGuard / NordLynx for the best speed and battery life.
  • DNS: default provider DNS or encrypted DNS; avoid public DNS that leaks.
  • Kill switch: on (block traffic without VPN).
  • Bypass list (split tunneling): keep banking and streaming apps inside the VPN unless they refuse to work.
  • Auto-connect: on boot and on unknown Wi-Fi networks.

See also: Best VPN Settings (UK).

Privacy check: leaks & kill switch

  1. Connect to the VPN → open IP/DNS leak test.
  2. Verify the shown IP is not your mobile/Wi-Fi IP and the DNS is from the VPN.
  3. Kill switch test: turn Airplane mode on/off — the app must reconnect before traffic flows.

Torrenting on Android

  • Use a provider with P2P-enabled servers and a no-logs policy.
  • Keep Kill switch on to avoid accidental IP leaks.
  • Prefer servers in the UK or nearby NL/DE for speed.

Full details in VPN for Torrenting (UK).

Fix slow speeds on Android

  • Switch server (same city first) or try a nearby country.
  • Toggle between WireGuard/NordLynx and OpenVPN UDP if your ISP meddles with UDP.
  • Disable battery savers for the VPN app so it stays connected.
