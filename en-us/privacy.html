2) What we collect

VPN World is built to minimize data collection. Depending on your choices and how you use the site, we may process:

  • Basic technical info (browser type, device type, approximate region/country, referral source) — typically via analytics, only if you opt in.
  • Usage info (pages viewed, time on page, high-level engagement events) — via GA4, only if you opt in.
  • Email content if you contact us (your email address and message text).

We do not run user accounts on this site and we do not intentionally collect sensitive categories of personal information.

3) Cookies and local storage

Cookies are small files stored on your device. VPN World uses cookies/local storage mainly for:

  • Consent preference: we store your analytics choice locally as vpnworld_consent.
  • Analytics cookies: only if you opt in to analytics.

If you opt in, VPN World uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand which guides are helpful, which pages need improvements, and how users move through the site (navigation, readability, structure).

  • Tool: Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

6) Global Privacy Control (GPC)

Some browsers and extensions support Global Privacy Control (GPC), a signal that expresses a preference to opt out of certain types of tracking. On VPN World (US), analytics is already off by default (opt-in).

In addition, if your browser sends a GPC signal, VPN World treats it as an opt-out request and keeps analytics disabled even if you previously opted in.

7) “Do Not Sell/Share” statement

VPN World does not sell your personal information. We also do not knowingly “share” personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising in the way it is typically defined in US state privacy laws.

If you opt in to analytics, GA4 may process identifiers/cookies used for measurement and reporting. You can opt out at any time.
