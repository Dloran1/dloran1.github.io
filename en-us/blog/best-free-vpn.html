Start with NordVPN Try Surfshark

Quick answer: free VPNs are fine for occasional, low-risk tasks, but expect data caps, smaller server pools, slower links and weaker streaming. For streaming, P2P, remote work or sustained privacy, a reputable paid plan is the safer bet.

Free is enough for…

  • Café/airport Wi-Fi hygiene (with our Wi-Fi safety checklist).
  • Occasional IP change for generic content (no streaming guarantees).
  • Learning basics — compare protocols and server choice.

Why paid plans win

  • Better chance against geo-blocks and more stable streaming.
  • Higher throughput — validate with a speed test.
  • Clearer no-logs posture and 24/7 support.

Safety tips for any free VPN

  1. Audited vendors, transparent privacy policy.
  2. Use Kill Switch — see Kill Switch.
  3. Test for leaks — DNS/IPv6 leak test.
  4. Understand rules — Is VPN legal?
