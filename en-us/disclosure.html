Disclosure & Affiliate Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2026

Transparency matters. This page explains how VPN World is funded, what affiliate links are, and what you can expect from our editorial standards.

What are affiliate links?

Some links on VPN World are affiliate links. If you click an affiliate link and later subscribe or purchase a service, we may earn a commission — at no additional cost to you.

Do affiliate links influence reviews?

Quick answer: No.

Affiliate relationships do not determine our conclusions. We focus on real-world behavior: privacy fundamentals, protocol choices, leak protection (DNS/IPv6/WebRTC), stability, and how services perform in typical scenarios like public Wi-Fi, remote work, and streaming.

Editorial independence

  • We do not accept payment for positive coverage.
  • We do not publish “guaranteed” sponsored rankings.
  • If a service performs poorly, we state it clearly and explain why.

Trademarks and third-party brands

All trademarks, product names, and logos referenced on this website are the property of their respective owners. Unless explicitly stated, VPN World is not officially associated with NordVPN, Surfshark, Netflix, Google, or any other third-party company.

Why we use affiliate revenue

Maintaining a testing workflow, running infrastructure, and keeping guides updated takes time and resources. Affiliate revenue helps fund testing and ongoing improvements while keeping the site accessible without a paywall.
